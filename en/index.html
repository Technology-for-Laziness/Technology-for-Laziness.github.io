We Make Programs Only for Your Laziness

We identify inconveniences and challenges in daily life.

We look closely at the small, often overlooked inconveniences people face every day, recognizing them not just as personal complaints but as collective social challenges that can and should be resolved through modern technology.

We build AI and code-based solutions to address them.

We combine the power of artificial intelligence and software engineering to develop practical tools, dedicating ourselves to continuous research and innovation until these technological ideas are fully realized to serve the public good.

All of our technologies are released as open source.

By making all of our source code completely open and accessible to the world, we strive to lower the barriers to technology, empower global developers, and ultimately contribute to building a more equitable and better society for everyone.
